## üÜò Troubleshooting

### GitHub Sponsors Issues
- **Not eligible**: Need 1+ year account history, or be in sponsored org
- **Stripe verification failed**: Check identity documents, contact support
- **Waiting on review**: Can take 1-2 business days, be patient

### Payment Issues
- **Payouts delayed**: Normal for first payment (up to 30 days)
- **Account holds**: Verify identity, complete any pending actions
- **Missing payments**: Check spam folder, contact platform support

### Technical Issues
- **Links not working**: Check .github/FUNDING.yml syntax
- **Products not showing**: Verify they're published, not draft
- **Profile not updating**: Clear cache, try different browser
